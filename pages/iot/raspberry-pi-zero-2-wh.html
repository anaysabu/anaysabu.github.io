A tiny single-board computer that is often underestimated — but extremely powerful in cybersecurity labs.

Why this board is underestimated

The Raspberry Pi Zero 2 WH looks small and simple, but it packs a quad-core CPU, onboard Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and USB OTG capabilities. Because of its size, low power consumption, and Linux support, it becomes a perfect stealth platform for security research, red-team labs, and hardware hacking experiments.
