Cybersecurity Awareness Session

Conducted at GHS West Kadungalloor • 28 Nov 2025 • By Anay Sabu VS & Ciya

About GHS West Kadungalloor

GHS West Kadungalloor was established in 1925 and is managed by the Department of Education, Kerala. The school is located in a rural area under the Aluva block of Ernakulam district. It is a co-educational institution offering classes from Grades 1 to 10, with an attached pre-primary section.

Session Overview

We conducted an interactive cybersecurity awareness class focusing on real-world threats, safe digital practices, and hands-on demonstrations. The students showed strong engagement and a positive response throughout the session.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction – Ethical standards
  • Social Media Security – App data, personal data, Snapchat compromise
  • Phishing Pages – Detection, social engineering, phishing lab
  • Strong Passwords – Weak vs strong, 2FA, creative passwords, hashes, session hijacking lab
  • Cyber Laws
  • Artificial Intelligence – What is AI, AI vs algorithms, AI jailbreaking lab
  • Dark Web
  • Wi-Fi Hacking
  • Security Gadgets
  • Trust & Device Safety – Why not to blindly trust computers & mobiles
